Contact Discovery
Find business emails, phone numbers, and key decision-makers without a ZoomInfo subscription.
  • Scrapes company websites, cross-references CRM email patterns, tests first-initial/last-name combinations
  • Each contact includes a confidence score (e.g., 90% deliverability estimate)
  • Multi-step fallback: website → CRM pattern match → web search → best guess with rationale
  • Integrates directly into buyer's list output as an additional column
  • Can also integrate with ZoomInfo if your firm has a subscription
Example Query
"For each buyer on this list, find the best email contact for the most relevant partner or deal lead. Include a confidence score and source."
